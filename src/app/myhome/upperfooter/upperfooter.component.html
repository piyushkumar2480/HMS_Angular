Our Hospital is a multi-speciality quaternary care hospital located at Pashan ,Pune in the western Indian state of Maharashtra. It is Maharashtra’s first National Accreditation Board for hospital (NABH) accredited hospital , and also India’s first HACCP and ISO : 2200:2005 certified hospital

To become partners in health promotion with every section of society.
